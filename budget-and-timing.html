PhD Proposal

5 Budget and Timing

Identify primary and secondary product forms using sea cucumber. Identify the quality measures that are important for sea cucumber used in these various product forms. Establish techniques for measuring quality at the laboratory and at the dockside. Standardize the quality measurement techniques developed using Newfoundland and Labrador harvested sea cucumber. Assess the current handling and holding techniques in the Newfoundland and Labrador Fishery at the wharf and in the production process using the developed standardized measures.

You should include an outline of the various stages and corresponding time lines for developing and implementing the research, including writing up your thesis. For full-time study your research should be completed within three years, with writing up completed in the fourth year of registration. For part-time study your research should be completed within six years, with writing up completed by the eighth year.
